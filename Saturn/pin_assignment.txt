Header P3 on Numato Saturn: 
Pins 5 - 48, 53 - 88 (80 pins total)
These pins go to the ZIF socket. 

Header P2 on Numato Saturn: 
Pins 7 - 8, 11 - 12, 15 - 44, 53 - 64 (46 pins total)
These pins go to the ZIF socket.

Remaining pins on Header P2: 
65 - 88 (24 pins)

Pins 65 - 66: RX/TX lines for BBB communication
Pins 67 - 71: Four clocks for counter ICs, one reset
Pins 72 - 76: Five output buffer signals
Pins 77:      Enable pin for voltage translators
Pins 78 - 86: One output enable pin (all SRAM ICs), four ~CS signals (one per two ICs), four ~WE signals (one per two ICs)
Pins 87 - 88: Not used
